Concept
	SpaceHunting is a game in which the player drives a spaceship to destroy a alien mothership before it runs away
Rules
	Win -> Destroy the mothership before it generate enough energy to run away
	Fail -> Be destroyed by the enemies
	How play -> The player drives the spaceship and shoot the enemies. The mothership only run away, generate energy and droids. The droids attack the player
Requirements
	- 3D Models of spaceships
	- Particles FX and sound FX for explosion, shoots, etc. 
	- Background music
	- Enemies simple AI
	- Play with Xbox360 pad
