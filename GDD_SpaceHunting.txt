Concept
	SpaceHunting is a game in which the player drives a spaceship and tries to run away to deep space while other spaceships (enemies) attack him
Rules
	Win -> Survive enough time to "jump" to speed light
	Fail -> Be destroyed by the enemies
	How play -> The player drives the spaceship, with six free grades of rotation and acceleration, and shoot the enemies
Requirements
	- 3D Models of spaceships
	- Particles FX and sound FX for explosion, shoots, etc. 
	- Background music
	- Enemies AI
	- Play with Xbox360 pad
